Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to allow the user to investigate weather. People who use this site are interested in more than just what to wear today, they are interested in looking at how weather occurs and why. These users are able to not only look at today's weather, but learn about weather phenomena, records and patterns that could affect where they live,where they want to live, or where they want to visit.
