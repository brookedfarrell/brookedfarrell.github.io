Adventures

1/2 Half Day Run

This half day run is perfect for an afternoon in the great outdoors. This option gives you a variety of experiences with some heavier rapids, calm waters and even a stop for swimming. Great for kids who haven't been down the river before and short enough for younger kids who might not need as much adventure. Snacks are provided as well as transportation back to our starting point.

Departure Times: 9:00 AM & 1:00PM

Monday through Saturday

Adults: $50 ☼ Youth (5-12): $35 ☼ Seniors (65+): $45

Full Day

Our full day package is great for serious adventurers who only have a day to get away. You will encounter heavier rapids with this run and it is more for an experienced adventurer. The full day package includes lunch as well as snacks and water and transportation back to our starting point.

Departure Times: 9:00 AM

Monday through Saturday

Adults: $100 ☼ Youth (5-12): $70 ☼ Seniors (65+): $90

Two Day with Camping

The weekend package is the best getaway for adventurers who want a little bit of everything. This package includes intense rapids, calm floating as well as an opportunity to kayak on some serious rapids. Two nights camping accomadation and meals are provided as well as transportation back to our starting point. This package is great for a weekend getaway with your friends or and exciting mini vacation for your family. Can be customized to your experience, but it is not for a beginner.

Departure Times: 9:00 AM Friday

Return Time: 5:00 PM Saturday

Adults: $300 ☼ Youth (5-12): $210 ☼ Seniors (65+): $270
